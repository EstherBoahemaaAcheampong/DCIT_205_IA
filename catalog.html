Course Catalog

Introduction to Computer Science

This foundational course introduces students to the fundamentals of computer science. Topics include programming concepts, problem-solving strategies, algorithm design, and an overview of computer hardware and software. Students will gain hands-on experience in coding and develop a solid understanding of the core principles that underpin the field of computer science. No prior programming experience is required.

Data Structures and Algorithms

This course delves into the design and analysis of fundamental data structures and algorithms. Topics include arrays, linked lists, trees, sorting algorithms, searching algorithms, and complexity analysis. Through practical assignments and projects, students will strengthen their problem-solving skills and gain proficiency in implementing efficient data structures and algorithms.

Web Development

Explore the world of web development in this hands-on course. Topics cover both front-end and back-end development, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, server-side scripting, and database integration. Students will work on real-world projects, gaining the skills needed to design and build dynamic, responsive, and interactive websites.

Digital and Logic systems

This course focuses on the principles of digital and logic circuits. Topics include Boolean algebra, combinational and sequential circuits, flip-flops, and finite state machines. Students will gain practical experience in designing and simulating digital circuits using industry-standard tools. The course provides a foundation for understanding the hardware aspects of computer systems.
